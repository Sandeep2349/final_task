Homestyle egg curry

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 3 spoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion ,finely chopped
  • 1/2 table spoon turmeric
  • 1 table spoon garam masala
  • fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and lightly fry the boiled egg until the outer skin is golden.
  2. In the same pan ,mix onions util translucent ,then add ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Pour in the tomato puree and add the turmeric and garam masala.
  4. Cook until the oil seperates from the masala ,then add a cup of water to create a gravy.
  5. Add the eggs to the gravy and simmer for 5 min.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.
